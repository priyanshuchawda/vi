# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project harassment-free for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in discussions and reviews.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Use inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or inflammatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers via:

- GitHub security contact: `SECURITY.md`
- Repository support channel: `SUPPORT.md`

Include links, timestamps, and any relevant evidence. Reports are handled as
confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments/PRs/issues that violate this
Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or
permanent contribution restrictions.
