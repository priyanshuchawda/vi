FAQ

Common questions

Is QuickCut really free?

Yes — completely free and open source under the MIT license. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden costs.

Does the AI require internet?

Core editing and local transcription (Vosk) work 100% offline. The AI assistant uses AWS Bedrock which requires internet and your own AWS credentials.

Is my footage private?

Yes. QuickCut is local-first. Your videos never leave your machine unless you explicitly upload to YouTube or AWS S3.

What video formats are supported?

QuickCut uses FFmpeg under the hood, so it supports virtually every video and audio format — MP4, MKV, MOV, AVI, WebM, and more.

Can I contribute?

Absolutely! QuickCut is open source. Check out CONTRIBUTING.md on GitHub to get started.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10+ or Ubuntu 18.04+. 8GB RAM recommended for smooth editing with large video files.
